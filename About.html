About Me

Hello I'm Delaram!

Education:

Simon Fraser University (2011-2016)

I am a graphic designer that enjoys working with imagery, colours, typefaces, illustration and grid layouts to form new and intersting communication methods. Graphic design allows me to unleash and engage with the power of communicaion through a fun, creative and often collaborative process.

I believe graphic design allows for the creative exploration of the mind. It allows for the showcase of thoughts and ideas, but also questions, analyzes, critiques and even creates miscommunication in order to express the interaction and conversations of our identities and societies

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, following different design streams/blogs and discovering new places, people and foods through travel.
